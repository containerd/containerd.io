containerd has graduated from the CNCF!

As of February 28, 2019, containerd is officially the fifth project to graduate from the Cloud Native Computing Foundation, behind Kubernetes, Prometheus, Envoy, and CoreDNS. We'd like to thank the amazing containerd community for making this all possible and we're excited for the future of the project.


You can read the official announcement on the CNCF website.
